Electricity Production and Consumption

How is electricity generated?



Electricity Consumption is exceeding its Production!

According to the General Authority of Statistics, mostly electricity consumption exceeds the electricity production through 1980 to 2016 as a result of the massive increase in the population of Saudi Arabia. Moreover, the demand for electricty is expected to increase by 6% in the next few years.

Distribution of Electricity Production

The Saudi electricity generation is heavily dependent on hydrocarbons, accounting for the following:

Crude oil: 29%

Diesel: 15%


Heavy fuel oil: 10%

Natural Gas: 49%

Distribution of Consumption

Residential: 50%

Industrial: 21%

Commercial: 15%

Government: 12%

Tips to save electricity during the day!
