Tips to save electricity during the day!

Car



  • - Heating the car
  • - Regular Maintenance
  • - Check the Tires Pressure

Air Conditioner



  • - Fix the tempriture between 23 and 25 degrees
  • - Close the windows when the AC is one
  • - Clean the AC's filter each two weeks

Lightening



  • - Use the sunlight as much as possible
  • - Divide the lightening devices on multiple switches
  • - Use automatics sensors

Insulation



  • - Use double glazing for the windows
  • - Paint the house with light colors
